## Part 2: Outreach Strategy by Tier

### Tier 1: High-Priority Outreach (Target: 3-5 links)
**Timeline:** Days 21-25
**Selection:** Just Landed, The Local, Spanish Propertynet, InterNations

**Strategy:**
1. Personalized research on each contact
2. Custom pitch highlighting mutual audience benefit
3. Offer multiple content options
4. Follow-up after 5-7 days if no response
5. Persistence: 3 total outreach attempts

### Tier 2: Secondary Outreach (Target: 5-10 links)
**Timeline:** Days 26-30
**Selection:** Additional lifestyle blogs, smaller expat resources, blogger outreach

**Strategy:**
1. Template-based outreach with personalization
2. Simpler asks (guest post, resource link)
3. Shorter pitch (personalization still key)
4. Follow-up after 10 days

### Tier 3: Ongoing Outreach (After Phase 1)
**Timeline:** Continuous
**Selection:** Media outlets, major publications, premium network sites

**Strategy:**
1. Build relationship first
2. Position as expert resource
3. Pitch newsworthy angles
4. Regular expert commentary contributions

## Part 4: Personalization Checklist

Before sending ANY outreach email, complete this checklist:

- [ ] **Research:** Spent 5+ minutes on target site
- [ ] **Specific Reference:** Mentioned specific article/section they've published
- [ ] **Audience Alignment:** Explained why your content matches THEIR audience
- [ ] **Value Proposition:** Clear on what YOU bring (not what you want)
- [ ] **Customized Subject:** Not a generic template subject
- [ ] **Contact Name:** Used actual person's name (not "Dear Editor")
- [ ] **Professional Tone:** Grammar/spelling checked
- [ ] **Call to Action:** Clear next step (meeting, submission, etc.)
- [ ] **Email Signature:** Professional with easy contact info

## Part 6: Tracking & Management

### Spreadsheet Columns
- Target Website
- Contact Name
- Email Address
- Outreach Date
- Template Used
- Status (Pending/No Response/Rejected/Accepted)
- Follow-up Dates
- Link/Article Published (if approved)
- Link Quality (DA, relevance rating)
- Date Published

### Status Categories
- **Pending:** Awaiting response
- **No Response:** 3x contact attempts exhausted
- **Rejected:** Explicitly declined
- **Accepted:** Agreement to publish
- **Published:** Link live and verified
- **Negotiating:** Back-and-forth discussion ongoing

### Success Metrics
- Response Rate Target: 20-30%
- Acceptance Rate Target: 40-50% of responses
- Publication Rate Target: 5-10 links in 10 days of Phase 1
- Average Link Quality: DA 25+

### Strong Success
- 50+ outreach emails sent

- 10+ links published or committed

- 75+ outreach emails sent

## Conclusion

Successful link outreach requires:
1. **Research:** Know your target's audience
2. **Personalization:** Custom pitch for each contact
3. **Value First:** Lead with what you offer, not what you want
4. **Persistence:** Follow up 2-3 times before moving on
5. **Tracking:** Document everything for future outreach

**Expected Outcome:** 5-15 quality backlinks from relevant Danish/Scandinavian expat and legal resource websites within 10 days, establishing credibility and improving SEO rankings significantly.

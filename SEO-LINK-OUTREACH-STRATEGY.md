- **Target:** 5-10 high-quality backlinks within 30 days

## Long-Term Relationship Building

After successful link acquisition:
- Continue relationship (regular engagement, social shares)
- Offer recurring content (monthly expert articles, quarterly features)
- Cross-promote each other's content
- Consider joint webinars or events

---

## Legal/Ethical Guidelines

✅ All outreach should be authentic and personalized
✅ Only pitch content that genuinely provides value
✅ Never offer payment for links (violates Google guidelines)
✅ Be transparent about who you are and what you do
✅ Respect "no" responses and move on
